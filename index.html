Thai boxing is a combat sport of Thailand that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "art of eight limbs" as it is characterized by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins.

Boxing is an ancient sport that dates back to Ancient Greece. The goal is to attack the opponent using a combination of fast and powerful punches above the waist to score points or knock the opponent out.
